Why El Paso Roofs Need Extra Care

Our desert climate is harsh on roofing materials

☀️ Intense UV Exposure

El Paso gets 300+ days of sunshine yearly. UV rays degrade shingles, sealants, and membranes faster than in other climates.

⛈️ Desert Hailstorms

Monsoon season (July-September) brings sudden, violent hailstorms that damage shingles and create impact vulnerabilities.

🌡️ Extreme Temperature Swings

Temperatures can swing from 30°F to 100°F+ in the same season, causing roofing materials to expand and contract repeatedly.

💨 Dust & Debris Accumulation

Dust storms and wind blow fine particles that trap moisture on your roof and clog gutters, accelerating decay.
