Frequently Asked Questions

Partner Program FAQ

How is referral tracking handled?
Each partner receives a unique referral ID or link upon signup. When your client contacts us, we log it to your account automatically. You can also call or email us with the referral details, and we'll manually tag it to your profile.
When do I get paid?
Referral commissions are paid within 7 business days of job completion. We offer payment via check or direct deposit — your choice. You'll receive an email notification when payment is processed.
Is the partnership exclusive?
Not at all. You're free to work with other contractors. We earn your referrals by delivering outstanding service to your clients — no lock-in contracts, no exclusivity requirements.
What territory do you cover?
We serve the greater El Paso metropolitan area including Upper Valley, Lower Valley, East El Paso, West El Paso, Northeast, Central, Horizon City, Socorro, Canutillo, Vinton, Anthony, and surrounding communities within 50 miles.
What support do partners receive?
All partners get priority scheduling, digital inspection reports, and a dedicated point of contact. Preferred and Elite partners also receive co-branded marketing materials, a dedicated phone line, and invitations to quarterly appreciation events.
Can I track my referral status?
Yes. We'll provide regular updates on every referral — from initial inspection through job completion. Elite partners also receive a monthly performance summary with stats on closed jobs, earnings, and pipeline status.
