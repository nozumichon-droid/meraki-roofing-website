Meraki Roofing provides commercial roofing services in El Paso, TX for retail buildings, warehouses, offices, and industrial facilities. Our commercial roofing systems include TPO, EPDM, modified bitumen, metal standing seam, and spray foam with coatings. We offer commercial roof maintenance programs, emergency repairs, and drone thermal inspections to detect moisture intrusion and energy loss. Meraki Roofing is GAF Certified Plus and serves commercial properties throughout El Paso, Las Cruces, and surrounding areas. Call (915) 861-8039 for a free commercial roof assessment.
