Frequently Asked Questions

Will filing a claim raise my premiums? ▼

In Texas, filing a weather-related claim (like storm damage) typically does not increase your premiums. Weather damage is considered "Acts of God" and most insurance companies will not penalize you for filing a legitimate claim. We recommend asking your insurance agent to confirm your specific policy details.

What if my claim is denied? ▼

If your claim is denied, you have options. We can review the denial letter and help you understand why. You can often file an appeal with your insurance company with additional documentation. If you believe the denial is unfair, you can contact the Texas Department of Insurance or consult with an insurance attorney. We're here to help you navigate this process.

Do I have to pay a deductible? ▼

Yes, typically you'll need to pay your deductible as outlined in your insurance policy. However, many homeowners end up paying little to nothing out-of-pocket because the insurance payout covers the deductible and the full cost of repairs. We work to maximize your insurance settlement to cover both the deductible and the repair costs.

How long does the process take? ▼

The typical process takes 3-4 weeks from initial inspection to approved claim, though it can vary. The timeline depends on your insurance company, the extent of damage, and how quickly adjuster appointments can be scheduled. We work to expedite the process and keep you informed at every step.

What if my roof has pre-existing damage? ▼

Insurance typically covers new damage caused by storms, not pre-existing wear and tear. However, if pre-existing damage contributed to the failure of your roof during the storm, it may be partially covered. Our detailed inspection will help identify what's storm damage versus pre-existing conditions, and we'll present this clearly to your adjuster.

Can I choose my own contractor? ▼

Yes, absolutely. You have the right to choose your contractor. We recommend choosing a licensed, insured, local contractor with experience filing insurance claims. This ensures your project is done right and you're working with someone who understands the insurance process. We're honored to be your choice for professional roofing work in El Paso.
