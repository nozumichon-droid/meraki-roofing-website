Frequently Asked Questions

How long does an inspection take? +

A typical roof inspection takes 20-30 minutes depending on the size and complexity of your roof. Our inspector will examine all accessible areas including the roof surface, flashing, gutters, vents, and attic ventilation.

Will the inspector go on the roof? +

Yes, whenever it is safe to do so. Our inspectors are trained and equipped to walk on roofs. If conditions are unsafe (extreme weather, very steep pitch), we use drones and high-resolution cameras to get a detailed view.

What if you find major damage? +

We will document everything with photos and provide you with a detailed report and repair or replacement options. If the damage may be covered by insurance, we can help you navigate the claims process. There is never any pressure to commit.

How long until I get a quote? +

You will receive your detailed report and exact quote within 24 hours of the inspection. For straightforward projects, we often have it ready the same day. The report includes photos, findings, material options, and itemized pricing.

Do you offer financing? +

Absolutely. We partner with multiple lenders to offer flexible financing options starting at 5.99% APR. Visit our financing page for details and current offers.

What areas do you service? +

We serve all of El Paso and surrounding communities including Horizon City, Socorro, San Elizario, Canutillo, Anthony, and Sunland Park. If you are unsure whether we cover your area, give us a call at (915) 861-8039.
