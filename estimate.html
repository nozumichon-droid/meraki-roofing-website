Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is this estimate? ▼

Our calculator uses current El Paso market data and provides a solid ballpark range. For exact pricing, we recommend a free on-site inspection where we can evaluate roof complexity, existing damage, and material availability.

Does this estimate include labor costs? ▼

Yes! Our pricing is fully installed and includes labor, materials, permits, and basic cleanup. Tear-off of existing roofing is an additional charge (shown in the breakdown).

What if my roof is under warranty or insurance covers the damage? ▼

Great question! Many insurance policies and warranties cover roofing work. We can help you navigate the claims process and work directly with your insurance company. Call us at (915) 861-8039 to discuss.

Do you offer financing? ▼

Absolutely. We work with several lending partners to offer flexible financing options. Visit our financing page for more details and current offers.

How long does installation take? ▼

Most residential roof installations take 1-3 days depending on size and complexity. Weather can affect the timeline. We'll provide a specific schedule when you book your free inspection.
