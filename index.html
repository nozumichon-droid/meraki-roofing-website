Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the questions El Paso homeowners ask us most about roofing.

How much does a roof replacement cost in El Paso?

The cost of a roof replacement in El Paso typically ranges from $5,000 to $15,000+ depending on the size of your home, roofing material selected, and complexity of the project. Asphalt shingles are the most affordable option, while natural slate runs higher. We provide free, itemized estimates with no hidden fees so you know exactly what to expect before any work begins.

Do you offer free roof inspections?

Yes — every roof inspection with Meraki Roofing is 100% free with no obligation. We'll assess the condition of your roof, identify any damage or areas of concern, and give you an honest recommendation. Whether you need repairs or not, we'll let you know exactly where you stand.

How do I know if my roof needs to be repaired or replaced?

Signs your roof may need attention include missing or curling shingles, visible leaks or water stains on ceilings, granules collecting in gutters, sagging areas, and age (most asphalt roofs last 20–25 years). During our free inspection, we'll assess the overall condition and let you know honestly whether a repair will do the job or if a full replacement makes more financial sense long-term.

Do you help with insurance claims for storm damage?

Absolutely. We work directly with your insurance company to make the storm damage claims process as smooth as possible. We document all damage thoroughly, provide detailed estimates that insurance adjusters need, and advocate on your behalf. Many El Paso homeowners have had their entire roof replaced at little to no out-of-pocket cost through insurance with our assistance.

What types of roofing materials do you work with?

We specialize in six types of roofing: asphalt shingles (the most popular choice), natural slate, flat roofing, foam roofing, single-ply membrane systems, and environmentally friendly options. Each material has different advantages in terms of durability, cost, and aesthetics. We'll help you choose the best option for your home, budget, and El Paso's climate.

How long does a roof replacement take?

Most residential roof replacements in El Paso are completed in 1–3 days, depending on the size of your home and the roofing material being installed. We'll give you a specific timeline during your estimate, and we always aim to minimize disruption to your daily routine. We also clean up thoroughly after every project — your property will look better than when we arrived.

Are you licensed, insured, and bonded?

Yes. Meraki Roofing is fully licensed, insured, and bonded in the state of Texas. We carry comprehensive general liability insurance and workers' compensation coverage, protecting both you and our crew. We're happy to provide proof of insurance upon request — just ask during your free inspection.

What areas do you serve in El Paso?

We serve the entire greater El Paso metropolitan area, including Northeast El Paso, West Side, Central, the Upper Valley, and surrounding communities. Whether you're in a newer development or an established neighborhood, our team is just a phone call away.
