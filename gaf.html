Workmanship Warranty

A GAF workmanship warranty covers costs associated with "misapplication" — leaks or damage caused by installation errors, not just material defects. Unlike standard contractor guarantees, these enhanced warranties are backed directly by GAF, meaning they'll honor the coverage even if your original roofing contractor goes out of business.
